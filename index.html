About As Flowers

As Flowers is a biannual literary magazine dedicated to publishing exceptional poetry, prose, and creative work that explores the human experience through the lens of natural beauty and emotional depth.

Founded in 2025 by Ashikur Rahman, we believe in amplifying diverse voices and creating space for experimental and traditional literary forms to coexist. Our name draws inspiration from the idea that words, like flowers, bloom in unexpected moments and transform the landscape of our inner worlds.

Each issue is carefully curated by our editorial team and features established and emerging writers from around the world.
